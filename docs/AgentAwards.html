My Awards (Agent View)

View and manage your performance recognitions and achievements

My Awards - Agent View

The Awards section is where you can view all the recognition you've received over time. This serves as your personal achievement gallery, showcasing your performance milestones and professional accomplishments.

Awards Overview​

Your awards represent recognition for excellent performance, skill development, and contribution to team success. Each award reflects specific achievements in your role and professional growth.

Viewing Your Awards​

Awards Table​

All your awards are displayed in an organized table format showing:

ColumnDescription
Award NameTitle of the recognition received
CategoryType of achievement (Performance, Training, Teamwork, etc.)
Date ReceivedWhen the award was granted
Awarded ByTeam lead or supervisor who granted the recognition
Achievement DetailsSpecific accomplishment that earned the award
Certificate StatusWhether a certificate is available for download
ActionsOptions to view details or download certificates

Award Categories​

Your awards are organized into different categories:

Performance Awards​

  • Top Performer: Exceptional overall performance
  • Customer Satisfaction Champion: Outstanding customer feedback scores
  • Quality Excellence: High-quality work consistently delivered
  • Efficiency Expert: Exceptional productivity and time management

Learning & Development Awards​

  • Quick Learner: Rapid skill acquisition and course completion
  • Knowledge Master: Comprehensive understanding demonstrated
  • Certification Achievement: Professional certifications earned
  • Continuous Improvement: Ongoing skill development

Team Collaboration Awards​

  • Team Player: Excellent collaboration and support
  • Mentor: Helping colleagues learn and grow
  • Innovation Contributor: Creative problem-solving and ideas
  • Leadership Potential: Demonstrating leadership qualities

Special Recognition​

  • Employee of the Month: Monthly outstanding performance
  • Milestone Achievement: Reaching significant career milestones
  • Problem Solver: Exceptional troubleshooting abilities
  • Customer Hero: Going above and beyond for customers

Searching and Filtering Awards​

Quick Search​

Use the search bar to quickly find specific awards by:

  • Award name or title
  • Achievement category
  • Keywords related to the accomplishment
  • Date ranges

Date Range Filter​

Filter awards by selecting a custom date range:

  1. Click on the Date Range selector
  2. Choose from preset options:
    • This Week
    • This Month
    • Last Month
    • This Quarter
    • This Year
  3. Or select custom start and end dates
  4. Click Apply to filter results

Category Filters​

Filter awards by category to view specific types of recognition:

  • Performance awards only
  • Learning achievements
  • Team collaboration recognitions
  • Special milestone awards

Sort Options​

Sort your awards by:

  • Date Received (newest first or oldest first)
  • Award Name (alphabetical order)
  • Category (grouped by type)
  • Awarding Authority (by supervisor or team lead)

Award Details​

Viewing Award Information​

Click View Award to open detailed information including:

  • Complete Award Description: Full details of the achievement
  • Performance Metrics: Specific data that earned the recognition
  • Achievement Context: Circumstances surrounding the award
  • Feedback Comments: Personal notes from your supervisor
  • Related Goals: Connection to your performance objectives

Award Certificates​

Downloading Certificates​

For awards that include certificates:

  1. Click View Award to open the award details
  2. Click Download Certificate button
  3. The certificate will download as a PDF file
  4. Save the file for your personal records

Certificate Features​

Your certificates include:

  • Official company branding and logo
  • Your name and employee details
  • Specific achievement description
  • Date of recognition
  • Digital signature from awarding authority
  • Unique certificate ID for verification

Uses for Certificates​

  • Personal Portfolio: Add to your professional portfolio
  • Performance Reviews: Include in self-assessment documents
  • Career Development: Use in job applications or promotions
  • Personal Records: Keep for your own achievement tracking

Award Notifications​

Receiving Award Notifications​

When you receive a new award:

  1. You'll receive an immediate in-app notification
  2. An email notification will be sent to your company email
  3. The award appears in your Awards section
  4. Your team lead may personally congratulate you

Notification Settings​

Manage how you receive award notifications:

  • In-app notifications (recommended)
  • Email notifications
  • SMS notifications (if configured)
  • Dashboard alerts

Personal Achievement Analytics​

Track your recognition patterns:

Frequently Asked Questions​

About Awards​

Q: How often are awards given? A: Awards are given based on performance and achievements, not on a fixed schedule. Some are monthly (like Employee of the Month), while others are given as specific accomplishments are achieved.

Q: Who can give awards? A: Awards are typically given by team leads, supervisors, managers, and department heads based on observed performance and achievements.

Q: Can I nominate myself for an award? A: While you cannot directly award yourself, you can highlight your achievements during performance reviews and one-on-one meetings with your supervisor.

About Certificates​

Q: Are all awards accompanied by certificates? A: Most formal awards include downloadable certificates. Some informal recognitions may not have certificates but are still recorded in your awards history.

Q: How long are certificates valid? A: Certificates do not expire and remain valid indefinitely as recognition of your achievement at that time.

Technical Support​

Q: What if I can't download my certificate? A: Try refreshing the page and clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact IT support or your team lead.

Q: Can I request a replacement certificate? A: Yes, contact your supervisor or HR department if you need a replacement certificate for any reason.

Celebrate Your Success! Your awards represent your hard work and dedication. Take pride in your achievements and use them as motivation for continued excellence. For questions about specific awards, contact your team lead or supervisor.
