# Stay Informed and Take Action

In a busy call center, important things happen fast. Vela's notification system keeps you in the loop without overwhelming you. Learn how to monitor alerts, respond to issues quickly, and keep your team connected when it matters most.

## What You Can Achieve

Notifications are your early warning system. Here's what you'll accomplish:

**Jump on problems fast** - Respond to customer issues before they become bigger problems
**Keep your finger on the pulse** - Stay informed about team performance and trends
**Work together better** - Collaborate through comments and discussions
**Track what matters** - Get automated reports and summaries that actually help
**Never miss a beat** - Stay on top of important events that affect your business

## Three Types of Notifications

### Alerts: Respond to Issues Immediately
Get notified about important events that require your attention (set up your alerts in [Smart Detector Overview](./smart-detector-overview.md)):

- **Smart Search matches** - Customer complaints or compliance issues detected
- **Performance alerts** - Agent scores below thresholds
- **System notifications** - Data processing complete or errors
- **Quality flags** - Potential issues with customer interactions

### Comments: Collaborate with Your Team
Stay updated on team discussions and feedback:

- **Agent feedback** - Comments on specific calls or interactions
- **Team discussions** - Questions and insights from colleagues
- **Management notes** - Important observations and instructions
- **Training feedback** - Suggestions for improvement

### Reports: Track Progress and Trends
Receive automated summaries of your team's performance:

- **Daily summaries** - Key metrics and highlights
- **Weekly reports** - Performance trends and insights
- **Monthly analytics** - Comprehensive team analysis
- **Custom reports** - Specific metrics you've requested

## Getting Started with Notifications

### Step 1: Access Your Notifications
1. **Log in** to your Vela dashboard
2. **Click "Notifications"** in the left sidebar
3. **Review the three tabs**:
   - **Alerts** (default) - Important events requiring attention
   - **Comments** - Team discussions and feedback
   - **Reports** - Automated summaries and analytics

![Notifications Overview](../img/screenshots/notifications-alerts.png)

### Step 2: Configure Your Preferences
1. **Go to "Settings" → "Notifications"**
2. **Choose your delivery method**:
   - Platform notifications (in-app)
   - Email notifications
   - Both for important alerts
3. **Set notification frequency**:
   - Immediate for critical alerts
   - Daily digest for general updates
   - Weekly summary for reports

## Managing Alerts Effectively

### Understanding Alert Types
**Smart Search Alerts**:
- Customer complaints detected
- Compliance violations flagged
- Quality issues identified
- Training opportunities spotted

**Performance Alerts**:
- Agent scores below thresholds
- Call quality issues
- Customer satisfaction drops
- Efficiency problems

### Taking Action on Alerts
1. **Click the eye icon** to view detailed information
2. **Investigate the issue** by reviewing the call or interaction
3. **Take appropriate action**:
   - Contact the customer if needed
   - Provide coaching to the agent
   - Update processes or training
   - Escalate to management
4. **Dismiss the alert** once resolved

### Setting Up Smart Alerts
1. **Go to "Smart Detector" → "Smart Search"**
2. **Create searches** for issues you want to monitor
3. **Set appropriate thresholds** for performance alerts
4. **Configure notification preferences** for each alert type

## Using Comments for Collaboration

### Adding Comments to Calls
1. **Go to "Calls"** and select a specific call
2. **Click "Add Comment"** in the call details
3. **Write your feedback** or observation
4. **Tag team members** if needed
5. **Save your comment** to notify the team

### Responding to Comments
1. **Check the Comments tab** regularly
2. **Click "View"** to see the full context
3. **Add your response** or take action
4. **Resolve the comment** when the issue is addressed

### Best Practices for Comments
- **Be specific** - Reference exact moments in calls
- **Be constructive** - Focus on improvement opportunities
- **Tag relevant people** - Ensure the right person sees it
- **Follow up** - Check that actions were taken

## Reviewing Reports and Analytics

### Understanding Report Notifications
When reports are generated, you'll receive notifications with:
- **Report title** and type
- **Time period** covered
- **Key findings** summary
- **Action options** (view, download, share)

### Accessing Your Reports
1. **Click "View"** to open the full report
2. **Review key insights** and trends
3. **Download as PDF** for sharing or archiving
4. **Share with stakeholders** as needed

### Setting Up Automated Reports
1. **Go to "Reports" → "Create"**
2. **Choose "Recurring Reports"**
3. **Set frequency** (daily, weekly, monthly)
4. **Select recipients** for automatic delivery
5. **Configure content** and format preferences

## Daily Notification Routine

### Morning Check (10 minutes)
1. **Review urgent alerts** - Address any critical issues
2. **Check new comments** - Respond to team feedback
3. **Review daily reports** - Understand yesterday's performance
4. **Plan your day** based on notifications

### Throughout the Day
1. **Monitor alerts** - Respond to issues as they arise
2. **Engage with comments** - Participate in team discussions
3. **Take action** on performance issues
4. **Update team** on important developments

### End of Day Review (15 minutes)
1. **Review all notifications** from the day
2. **Ensure all alerts** have been addressed
3. **Follow up** on any pending actions
4. **Plan tomorrow's priorities**

## Best Practices for Notification Management

### Prioritize Your Alerts
- **Critical alerts** - Respond immediately
- **Important alerts** - Address within 24 hours
- **Informational alerts** - Review during scheduled time

### Use Filters Effectively
- **Filter by type** - Focus on specific alert categories
- **Filter by team** - Review alerts for your specific team
- **Filter by date** - Look at recent or historical notifications

### Set Up Escalation Procedures
- **Define response times** for different alert types
- **Assign responsibilities** for different issues
- **Create escalation paths** for unresolved issues
- **Document procedures** for common scenarios

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Too Many Notifications
**Problem**: Receiving too many alerts or irrelevant notifications
**Solutions**:
- Adjust Smart Search criteria to be more specific
- Modify notification preferences to reduce frequency
- Set up filters to focus on important alerts
- Review and refine alert thresholds

### Missing Important Notifications
**Problem**: Not receiving alerts for critical issues
**Solutions**:
- Check notification settings and delivery preferences
- Verify Smart Search configurations are active
- Ensure email notifications aren't going to spam
- Test notification system with a sample alert

### Notification Delivery Issues
**Problem**: Notifications not appearing or delayed
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection and browser settings
- Verify email settings and spam filters
- Refresh the page or restart the application
- Contact support if issues persist
